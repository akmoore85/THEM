Julien Menzine

Julien was a top soldier in the army. Capable, efficient, and judiciously ruthless, He was selected to participate in the testing of a new nanomachine bioenhancement serum. The initial results of the nanites were underwhelming, but after leaving the service and interacting with the S.U.I.T, their true capabilities were revealed. His natural talents combined with Army experience make him the co-leader of T.H.E.M
